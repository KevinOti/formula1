Formula One¶

Formula one is one sport that attracts many, back in the days when television was the main source of news, a good number followed through with the sports news to learn how their favorite driver and constructor team faired in the various grand prix races and also who was on cause to win the title

The formula one website has all the data dating back to year 1950, it would be interesting to learn how the competition has transitioned over the years, the same

One of the skills that analyst must poses is even how to extract data, ahead of transforming and perfroming the necessary analysis
